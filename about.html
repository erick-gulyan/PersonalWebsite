About Me!

Here are some of my projects!

STEM Club Mobile Application

I was a part of STEM Club at Glendale Community College, where we worked to create an app which allowed students to navigate around campus with ease. The app included a points system for answering questions which other students may post and a sophisticated map system which allowed students to find their classes. I was a lead programmer on this application, which was created in a combination of Javascript, HTML, and utilizing a Firebase database.

Stock Market Discord Bot

I am part of a team in the Computer Science and Engineering Society at UC San Diego creating a discord bot to mimic a real-life stock market. Our bot is mainly programmed in Javascript using Node.js and Discord.js. We also incorporated a Firebase database to store user information as well as update stock values in a different subset of our database. The bot allows for buying, selling, and viewing your personal portfolio as well as the server leaderboard.
